DRURY'S CAFE

Drury's cafe is the brainchild of former commentator Peter Drury. The cafe offers simple yet exquisite meals to suit a range of palates. Experience dining with a difference as you order from our menu. All meals come with a complimentary glass of wine or soft drink. Meat is served medium rare or well-cooked. Salads come with a dressing of your choosing.
